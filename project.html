STEMify

This page gives an outline on what the project STEMify is all about.The project focuses on creating an engaging platform that promotes STEM learning through interactive modules, community collaboration, and practical challenges for students.

Project Overview

The STEMify project aims to design and develop a responsive web application that offers modular learning paths in Mathematics, Computing, Physics, and Chemistry. The platform will include interactive exercises, a resource library, and team-based challenge events to foster collaboration and hands-on experience.

Project Objectives

  • To give information on different degrees
  • To assist undergraduate students to see which career path better suits them and the degree they are pursuing
  • To give students a wider options of careers available to them based on the degree they have obtained

Key Features

Search Bar

A search engine that allows users to discover various Universities and courses.

Quizzes

Quizzes that help students identify their stregnths , interests, and personality traits and match them withtheir potential STEM career paths.

Glossary

A page that contains everday complex terms that students might not be familiar with and their explanations.
